About Us

We are the Entrepreneurship Cell of Saffrony Institute of Technology, dedicated to nurturing creativity and entrepreneurial mindset among students. Our mission is to provide resources and opportunities to help aspiring entrepreneurs turn their ideas into successful ventures.

Mission

To inspire, educate and empower engineering students and faculty to inculcate a culture of entrepreneurship and develop skills to solve real world problems for a positive change in society.

Vision

To be a leading E-Cell in Gujarat that fosters innovation and social impact through excellence in teaching, research and outreach by facilitating best of class resources & techniques in entrepreneurship.

Compendium

Dandelions, one of the beautiful flowers, need a push for their seeds to disperse so that one more child may smile upon seeing it. We, the members of Entrepreneurship Cell of Saffrony Institute of Technology, lookout for the seed (capable of contributing to a greater extent to those in need) among the youngsters with passion, ambition and will to achieve their dream. Just as the gust, We look out for them and provide them with the knowledge and guidance so that when the end of this journey approaches, they discover that this is just the beginning.

Our Aim

At E-Cell SIT, we assert that there exists a deficiency in exposure to the societal aspects of entrepreneurship, or entrepreneurship in general, within Engineering College. Even if there are students harboring specific ideas they wish to pursue, the prevailing culture and demanding timetable of Engineering college might stifle the inclination to embark on something new. The fundamental inquiry that arises is:'Where to Commence?' A widely held notion characterizes us as 'The Leaders for Tomorrow'. E-Cell, however, subscribes to the concept of being 'The Leaders for Today'. Our objective is to nurture the requirements of youthful intellects and their concepts while actively seeking transformative change. As aspiring entrepreneurs originating from legal backgrounds, we endeavor to advocate for a culture of embracing risk and the associated principles, with the aspiration of effecting meaningful change. The overarching goal is to establish an environment conducive to the acquisition of entrepreneurial knowledge, particularly tailored for individuals with an exclusive legal background.

Our Achievements

Celebrating our milestones and successes in fostering entrepreneurship

E-Summit

Achieved 18th rank in the National Entrepreneurship Challenge 2023 and were invited to IIT Bombay for further competitions, where our entire team participated in the E-Summit.

Entrepreneur Mentorship

Hosted ProTalks, where we invited CEOs Shyam Hathalia and Hardik Tarpara to mentor our members, leading to successful placements for several batchmates.

Eureka 2024

In Eureka 2024, our top two teams, Team Pixel and Team ArogyaMitra, qualified at the zonal level and went on to compete at IIT Bombay.

Socializing in corporate world

Conducted a LinkedIn Optimization Session that helped many of our batchmates and juniors enhance their profiles, earning them positive feedback and increased visibility.

Eureka 2023

In Eureka 2023, our winning team, Team Vidhyut, advanced to compete with top state teams at IIT Bombay.
